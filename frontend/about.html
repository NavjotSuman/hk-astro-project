    How can astrology help me in my career decisions?

    Astrology can provide insights into your strengths, weaknesses, and career potential based on your birth chart. By understanding the planetary influences affecting your professional life, you can make informed decisions and pursue paths aligned with your cosmic blueprint.

    What is Manglik Dosha, and how does it affect marriage?

    Manglik Dosha is a astrological condition caused by the position of Mars in certain houses of the birth chart. It's believed to bring challenges in marriage, such as conflicts or delays. However, with proper analysis and remedies, Manglik Dosha can be mitigated, ensuring a harmonious marital life

    Can you help me understand my Kundli Dosha and suggest remedies

    Kundli Dosha refers to planetary combinations in the birth chart that may cause obstacles or challenges in life. Through detailed analysis, I can identify specific Doshas affecting you and provide personalized remedies, such as gemstone recommendations, mantra chanting, or ritual practices, to alleviate their effects.

    What insights can I gain from a name analysis?

    A name analysis can reveal hidden aspects of your personality, talents, and potential based on the vibrations associated with your name. By understanding these influences, you can gain clarity about your strengths and challenges, empowering you to make conscious choices and maximize your opportunities for growth and success.
