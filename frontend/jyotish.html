    Welcome to Jyotish

     

    Jyotish, also known as Vedic astrology, is an ancient system of astrology originating in India. It is based on the belief that the positions and movements of celestial bodies, such as planets and stars, can influence human affairs and natural phenomena.

    Practitioners of Jyotish may use various techniques, such as dasha systems (cycles of planetary periods), muhurta (auspicious timing), and remedial measures (such as gemstone therapy, mantra recitation, and rituals) to mitigate negative influences and enhance positive ones.It's important to note that while Jyotish astrology is deeply ingrained in Indian culture and spirituality, its validity and effectiveness are subject to debate and skepticism, much like other forms of astrology.
